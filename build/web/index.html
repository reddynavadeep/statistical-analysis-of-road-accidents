ABOUT

The number of fatalities due to road accidents in India has been consistently above one lakh every year. Although the fatality index and rates are declining the actual figure is still at an alarming stage. In order to help in reducing the number of road accidents and fatalities, the availability of reliable and comprehensive road accident database and analysis system is critical to understand the pattern of accident causation.
